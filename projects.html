Projects

Code for Ghana runs a number of open data projects. We believe that open data in the hands of innovative individuals has the potential to cause a change in the country by way of making leaders more accountable to citizens and citizens more involved in governance. This is why we do a lot of advocacy work through data storytelling and build the capacity of young tech-enthusiasts to analyze and visualize data.
